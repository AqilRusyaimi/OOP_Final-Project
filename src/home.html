Best destinations for skydiving in Asia

Asia has a plethora of destinations for skydiving, each offering a unique experience. Here are some of the best destinations for skydiving in Asia:

Skydiving in Thailand

Thailand is a popular destination for skydiving, with the beaches of Pattaya being a hot spot for adrenaline junkies. You can enjoy a tandem skydive from 13,000 feet, which takes around 60 seconds of freefall. During the jump, you will have a breathtaking view of the Gulf of Thailand and the lush green landscape.

Skydiving in the Philippines

The Philippines is a hidden gem for skydiving, with its beautiful islands and crystal-clear waters. You can experience the thrill of skydiving in Subic Bay, which is located in the northwest of Manila. The jump takes place at 10,000 feet, and you will have a stunning view of the bay area and the surrounding mountains.

Skydiving in China

China is a vast country with diverse landscapes, and it offers several skydiving locations. One of the popular destinations is Zhangjiajie, known for its natural beauty and the inspiration for the Avatar movie. You can take a tandem jump from 12,000 feet and enjoy the breathtaking view of the national park.

Skydiving in India

India is a land of diversity and culture, and it offers several destinations for skydiving. The Aamby Valley in Maharashtra is a popular destination for skydiving, offering a unique experience of jumping over the picturesque Sahyadri Mountains. You can enjoy a tandem jump from 10,000 feet and experience the thrill of freefall for about 30 seconds.

Skydiving in Malaysia

Malaysia is a tropical paradise that offers a unique experience of skydiving over the stunning Langkawi islands. You can jump from 10,000 feet and enjoy the stunning views of the turquoise waters, white sandy beaches, and the lush green forests.

Skydiving in Japan

Japan is known for its rich culture, tradition, and technological advancements, and it offers a unique skydiving experience in the city of Ibusuki. You can enjoy a tandem jump from 12,000 feet and witness the breathtaking views of the city, the ocean, and the famous active volcano, Sakurajima
